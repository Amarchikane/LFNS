Our Programs

Preschool Program

Our preschool program is designed for children ages 2-4 and focuses on developing foundational skills such as communication, socialization, and problem-solving. We offer a play-based curriculum that encourages exploration and creativity.

Kindergarten Program

Our kindergarten program is designed for children ages 5-6 and prepares them for elementary school. We offer a balanced curriculum that includes language arts, math, science, social studies, and more. Our experienced teachers provide individualized attention and support to help each child succeed.
