About LFNS Nursery School

LFNS Nursery School has been providing high-quality education to young children for over 20 years. Our mission is to help each child reach their full potential in a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment.

At LFNS Nursery School, we believe that every child is unique and has their own strengths and interests. That's why we offer a wide range of programs and activities to cater to each child's individual needs.

We are proud to have a team of experienced and dedicated teachers who are passionate about early childhood education. Our teachers are trained in the latest teaching methodologies and are committed to creating a positive learning experience for your child.

Our Programs

Preschool

Our preschool program is designed for children aged 2-4 years old. We provide a play-based curriculum that focuses on social, emotional, and cognitive development.

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program is designed for children aged 4-5 years old. We provide a comprehensive curriculum that prepares children for the transition to primary school.
